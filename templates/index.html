Filming Tips for Best Results

  • Stand or walk directly facing away from the camera (posterior view).
  • Ensure good lighting and a plain background.
  • Keep the entire body in the frame from head to ankles.
  • For walking, move slowly and steadily away from the camera.
  • Wear fitted clothing so body contours are visible.
